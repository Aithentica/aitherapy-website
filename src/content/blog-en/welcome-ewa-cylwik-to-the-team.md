A warm welcome to the team for **Ewa Cylwik**!

Since February 2026, Ewa has been making sure Therapy Support speaks your language — the language of therapists who work with people, not with technology.

## What does Ewa do?

Ewa makes sure that every message, instruction, and feature description is:

- **Clear** — free of unnecessary technical jargon
- **Helpful** — showing how things work in everyday practice
- **Consistent** — whether you're reading an email, a webpage, or an in-app message

Her mission? To make working with the platform intuitive. So you always know what to do, without guessing.

## What's changing?

Over the coming months, Ewa is working on:

- **Clearer in-app messages** — no more guessing what buttons do
- **Better onboarding** — from registration to your first session, step by step
- **Help that works** — answers to your questions before you even ask them

All so you can focus on what matters most — working with your patients.

## Why Ewa?

We needed someone who understands that good communication isn't just a nice-to-have. It's a fundamental part of a tool you use every day. Someone who knows that clear messaging isn't optional — it's a requirement for the platform to truly support you.

Ewa is exactly that person. Welcome aboard!
